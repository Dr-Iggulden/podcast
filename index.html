About the Podcast

Clinical intelligence.
No compromise.

Most natural health podcasts are run by people who read about medicine. This one is run by someone who practises it.

The Precision Health Podcast delivers evidence-based insight into herbal medicine, integrative health, longevity science, and the pharmacognosy of medicinal plants. Every episode draws on active clinical experience and primary literature. This is not a wellness podcast. It is a clinical one.

Hosted by Dr Luke Iggulden from Precision Natural Medicine in Queensland, Australia, each episode is built for people who are done with surface-level health content and ready to understand the mechanism behind the medicine.

What We Cover

Herbal & Botanical Medicine

Phytotherapy, pharmacognosy, and the clinical evidence behind medicinal plants. From ethnobotanical tradition to modern application.

Longevity & Healthy Ageing

The science of healthspan. Evidence-based strategies for ageing with vitality, function, and independence on your own terms.

Biohacking & Optimisation

Nutritional Medicine
